About Me

I’m a graphic designer based in Helsinki. I’m currently working as a freelancer and looking for job opportunities.

I grew up in Kiviniemi, a small port town near the city of Oulu. After graduating from Upper Secondary School I began studying visual culture in Oulu University of Applied Sciences. I studied graphic design and participated in Oulu Game Lab during my studies. I learned a lot about making games and even took part in making a published mobile game State of Matter.

After graduating as a Bachelor of Media and Arts, I moved to Helsinki. My aim is to craft beautiful and functional designs. I’m skilled in graphic design, layout design and digital illustrations.

Currently I’m immersing in web design and learning how to code.
