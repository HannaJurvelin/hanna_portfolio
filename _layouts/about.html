About Me

I’m a graphic designer based in Helsinki. I’m currently studying IT at Laurea University of Applied sciences. I also work as a freelancer.

I grew up in Kiviniemi, a small port town near the city of Oulu. After graduating from Upper Secondary School I began studying visual culture in Oulu University of Applied Sciences. I studied graphic design and participated in Oulu Game Lab during my studies. I learned a lot about making games and even took part in making a published mobile game State of Matter.

After graduating as a Bachelor of Media and Arts, I moved to Helsinki. My aim is to craft beautiful and functional designs. I’m skilled in graphic design, layout design and digital illustrations. I wish to be a great UI/UX-designer in the future.

Contact me about freelancer work or job opportunities/trainee positions as a UI/UX-designer.
